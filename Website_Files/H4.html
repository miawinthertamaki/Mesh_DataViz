Connecting Highs and Lows: Physical Infrastructure

Infrastructure is often far more ambitious than immediate realities. NYC Mesh, by nature of running on a volunteer-based labor structure, is hindered in its ability to connect all of its nodes in a timely fashion. This can lead to “phantom nodes” that have either been installed but not connected, requested but not installed, or otherwise poorly documented and barely in existence. Of course, given the aspirations of NYC Mesh to create a more egalitarian internet system, great ambition here is no great tragedy!
